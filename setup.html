Set up on a solid door that can support your body weight. Ideally you want to workout on the side of the door that pushes away from you, though solid doors with a dead bolt as in the video work great too. Do not use on flimsy hollow core doors or metal doors with sharp edges. Our current version works on doors between 6’7 and 7’1. Units for taller doors can be custom ordered.
